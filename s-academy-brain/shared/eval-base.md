# Base Evaluation Instructions

Use this shared evaluation logic across S.Academy courses.

## Evaluation principles

- Score based on understanding, not exact wording.
- Accept equivalent phrasing when the learner shows the right concept.
- Keep feedback short and actionable.
- Do not introduce new concepts that were not part of the course unless needed for clarification.

## Default scoring scale

| Score | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 3 | Correct and clear |
| 2 | Mostly correct, minor gaps |
| 1 | Partially correct, major gaps |
| 0 | Incorrect or not attempted |

## Required feedback output

```text
Score:
Feedback:
Correct answer:
Improvement tip:
```
